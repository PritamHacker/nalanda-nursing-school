A.N.M Admission Instruction

  1. Certificates for Admission :-
    • Admit Card (Matriculation and Intermediate).
    • Mark Sheet (Matriculation and Intermediate).
    • Provisional Certificate.
    • Aadhar card.
    • 5 Passport size colour Photo
  2. Certificates for Scholarship :-
    • Residential certificate.
    • Caste certificate.
    • Income certificate.
    • Aadhar card.
    • Government Bank Account Passbook.
    Note:- Compile a Shadow Copy (Photo Copy) of all Certificates with the entry form.
  3. School Uniform :-
    • White Suit.
    • White Salwar.
    • White Scarf.
    • White Cap.
    • White Shoe and Socks.
    Prescribed uniform is compulsory for every student.
